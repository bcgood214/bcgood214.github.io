Ace Supply, Inc. is an Alaskan owned and operated petroleum equipment sales company. Ace was established in 1962 by a very ambitious young woman who began selling equipment to enhance the expanding ACE FUEL delivery company and Standard Service Station that she was already operating. The boom of the Alaska pipeline spurred the shift from ACE FUEL into the next level of equipment sales and supply for the Great Land.
